# Question
A Carnot engine operates between a hot reservoir at $500\text{ K}$ and a cold reservoir at $300\text{ K}$. What is the thermal efficiency of the engine?

# Options
- **A**: $40\%$
- **B**: $60\%$
- **C**: $50\%$
- **D**: $20\%$

# Explanation
The efficiency $\eta$ of a Carnot engine is given by $\eta = 1 - \frac{T_C}{T_H} = 1 - \frac{300}{500} = 0.4$ or $40\%$.
